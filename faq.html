Support Center

Common Questions

How long does delivery take?
Deliveries within KZN typically take 1-3 business days. For the rest of South Africa, it's 3-5 business days. International shipping times vary by location, usually between 7-14 days.
Are your fragrances authentic?
Yes, 100%. We pride ourselves on crafting and sourcing only real, high-quality fragrance oils and blends. Every bottle is verified for quality and longevity.
Can I track my order?
Absolutely. Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can also use our "Track Order" page to see real-time status.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for unopened and unused products. Due to hygiene reasons, we cannot accept returns on opened fragrance bottles.

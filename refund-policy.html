Refund Policy

At ISPHILO FRAGANCE, we strive for perfection in every scent. Due to the personal and hygienic nature of our products, we maintain a strict returns policy to ensure the safety and satisfaction of all our clients.

1. Returns Eligibility

Returns are only accepted for products that are damaged upon arrival or if the incorrect item was shipped. To be eligible, items must be unused, in their original luxury packaging, and reported within 48 hours of delivery.

2. Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed to your original method of payment within 7-10 business days.

3. Exclusions

Sale items, opened fragrances, and personalized scents are strictly non-refundable. We recommend trying our sample sets before committing to a full-size fragrance.

4. Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the client, except in cases where the product was received damaged or incorrect.

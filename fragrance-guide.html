The Essence Guide

Understanding Fragrance Notes

  • Top Notes: The initial impression. Fresh and light, these scents evaporate within 15-30 minutes.
  • Heart Notes: The core essence. These emerge as top notes fade, lasting for 2-4 hours.
  • Base Notes: The lasting signature. Deep and rich, these linger for 6-12+ hours.

Application Secrets

  • Pulse Points: Apply to wrists, neck, and behind ears where blood flow is strongest.
  • Don't Rub: Rubbing your wrists together breaks down the fragrance molecules too fast.
  • Hydrated Skin: Fragrances last significantly longer on well-moisturized skin.

Our Heritage

A Legacy of Elegance

Based in the vibrant heart of Durban, Isphilo Fragrances is a premier cosmetic brand specializing in a sophisticated range of fragrances for both men and women.

Each scent is meticulously crafted from distinctive blends of essential oils and other fine ingredients, resulting in long-lasting fragrances designed to linger for hours on end. Our signature collections are available in elegant 50ml and 30ml bottles, offering a perfect fusion of luxury and longevity.
